Feel free to Explore

Access the explore page to see random attractions around you.
You can save any of these places for the next Tour.

Generate your Tour

Open the Start Tour tab wherever you want to generate a tour from, configure all the details and tap the save button.
You can always customize the tour after it's generated by skipping, replacing or adding checkpoints.

View your Profile

Open the profile page to go trough a feed of your completed tours starting with the most recent ones. You can also change profile information like profile picture, name and other information.
